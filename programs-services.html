Programs & Services

Personalized programs designed to meet diverse needs.

Music Therapy Services are currently available in our Clinton office, in your home, or in your facility.

Private Individual or Small Group Music Therapy

The music therapist provides individual or small group sessions, as appropriate, to support each person's unique needs, including the development of social, motor, communication, academic, and emotional skills.

Early Childhood Music Recreation Classes

Sing, Dance, Play! Make music with your child. Open to children under 5 of all abilities and their caregiver. Classes are offered in blocks - Follow us on Facebook and Instagram for dates and time!

Piano/Guitar Lessons
Adapted Music Lessons

Individual music lessons that can be adapted for clients with special needs. The goal of adapted lessons may or may not be musical, e.g. vocal lessons to work on communication goals or piano lessons to improve fine motor coordination.

Contract Services

Contract services available to facilities. Please contact for availability and pricing.

Entertainment

Group entertainment available for your facility both in person or virtually utilizing Zoom. Contact for details and pricing.

Music Therapy Presentations

Presentations by Dana Prouse, Music Therapist, for your workplace or organization are available.
