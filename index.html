Support Through the Power of Music

Whether your child is struggling with communication, a loved one is experiencing memory changes, or you’re seeking a supportive outlet for emotional expression, music therapy offers a safe and engaging approach to growth and healing.

No prior musical experience is required — just a willingness to participate.

Who Can Benefit from Music Therapy?

Children & Youth

Support for speech and language development, emotional regulation, social skills, and academic engagement.

Adults

Assistance with stress reduction, mental health support, cognitive stimulation, and recovery from injury or illness.

Seniors

Meaningful engagement for seniors experiencing dementia, memory challenges, mobility limitations, or social isolation.
